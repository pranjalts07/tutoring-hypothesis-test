# Attributes for both student-mat.csv (Math course) datasets:

1. sex - student's sex (binary: "F" - female or "M" - male).
2. age - student's age (numeric: from 15 to 22).
3. Medu - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education).
4. Fedu - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education).
5. studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours).
6.failures - number of past class failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4).
7. freetime - free time after school (numeric: from 1 - very low to 5 - very high).
8. absences - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93).

# these grades are related with the course subject, Math:

G3 - final grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
