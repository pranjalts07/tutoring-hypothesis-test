# Attributes for both student-mat.csv (Math course) datasets:

1. paid - extra paid classes within the course subject (Math or Portuguese) (binary: yes or no)
2. studytime - weekly study time (numeric: 1 - <2 hours, 2 - 2 to 5 hours, 3 - 5 to 10 hours, or 4 - >10 hours).
3. Medu - mother's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education).
4. Fedu - father's education (numeric: 0 - none,  1 - primary education (4th grade), 2 – 5th to 9th grade, 3 – secondary education or 4 – higher education).
5.failures - number of past class failures (numeric: n if 1<=n<3, else 4).
6. absences - number of school absences (numeric: from 0 to 93).
7. G3 - final grade (numeric: from 0 to 20, output target)
